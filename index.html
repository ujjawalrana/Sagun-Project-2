The SAGUN Legacy | Since 1999

Built on Experience.
Driven by Industrial Excellence.

SAGUN is a leading manufacturer of industrial and edible grade acid casein in India, recognized for consistent quality and reliable bulk supply.

With roots in dairy since 1935 and a focused expansion into casein manufacturing in 1999, we have played a key role in transforming Morena into the “Casein Capital of India”. Today, SAGUN serves multiple industries across domestic and international markets.

Our facility combines deep-rooted dairy expertise with modern processing systems to deliver products that meet the rigorous standards of global manufacturers ensuring precision, consistency, and reliability in every batch.
